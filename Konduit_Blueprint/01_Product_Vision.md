# Konduit Product Vision

## Mission
Konduit is an Agency Intelligence Platform rather than a project manager.

It exists to:
- Coordinate agency operations.
- Translate technical work into client-friendly language.
- Improve agency profitability.
- Improve client transparency.
- Predict operational risks before they occur.

## Core Principles

- Operational Intelligence over task management.
- Continuous awareness rather than event dependence.
- Client clarity over technical complexity.
- AI augments humans; it does not replace decision makers.
- Hidden technical complexity.
- Official APIs and supported imports only.
- Multi-tenant SaaS.
